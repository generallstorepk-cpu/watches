Craftsmanship Experience

From raw metal
to masterpiece.

Five stages, eleven months on average, zero shortcuts. This is the journey every Vernay case and movement takes before it reaches a wrist.

01

Design & Sketch

Each model begins as a hand-drawn elevation, refined for months before a single component is milled.

02

Movement Assembly

Up to 280 components are assembled by a single watchmaker under magnification, by hand.

03

Hand Finishing

Côtes de Genève striping and bevelled edges are applied entirely by hand — no machine reproduces it.

04

Quality Testing

Each movement runs for 15 days across five positions, logged daily to within fractions of a second.

05

Final Inspection

Our master watchmaker signs the case back personally — the last human check before it ships.

Watch Guide

Living with
a mechanical watch.

Care Tips

  • Wind a hand-wound watch at the same time daily for steadiest accuracy
  • Keep automatic pieces away from strong magnets and speakers
  • Service the movement every 4–5 years, even if it's still running well
  • Wipe the case and bracelet weekly with a dry microfibre cloth

Buying Guide

  • Decide between hand-wound, automatic, or hybrid smart movement first
  • Case size: 38–40mm reads classic, 42mm+ reads sport or contemporary
  • Check water resistance against how you'll actually wear the piece
  • Ask for the movement reference — it tells you exactly what's inside

Collection Recommendations

  • First mechanical watch: start with the Classic Collection
  • Daily, do-everything piece: the Swiss Collection diver-chronometer
  • Investment-grade pieces: Limited Edition, numbered and certified
  • Boardroom plus gym: the Smart Collection hybrid
Questions

Frequently asked.

How accurate is a Vernay mechanical movement?

Every movement is chronometer-tested to within -4/+6 seconds per day before it leaves the atelier — well inside official Swiss chronometer standards.

How often does a Vernay watch need servicing?

We recommend a full service every 4–5 years. Your boutique will track service history against the case serial number automatically.

What's included in the lifetime mechanical warranty?

Movement repair or replacement for manufacturing defects, for as long as you own the piece, honoured at any Vernay boutique worldwide.

Can I authenticate a Limited Edition piece?

Yes — every Limited Edition case back is laser-engraved with a unique number that we can verify against our production ledger on request.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship insured to over 40 countries. Duties and import taxes are calculated at checkout so there are no surprises on delivery.

Is the Smart Collection compatible with iOS and Android?

Yes. Connect Titanium pairs with both via the Vernay companion app, with battery life rated up to 12 days per charge.
